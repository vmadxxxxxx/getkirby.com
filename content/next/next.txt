Title: Kirby Next

----

Intro: Kirby has been running successfully for more than five years and we managed to release two major versions with dozens of releases to thousands of users world-wide. Now it's time for the next step! 

----

Text:

## We need your support

We started working on Kirby 3 a few months ago and we already made great progress, but we need your help to reach our goal. Kirby has become a sustainable full-time job for Bastian, but there's still a very limited bugdet for the other team members. With your help, we could manage to spend more time on Kirby together and release Kirby 3 faster. 

## Follow our journey

We want you to take part in our journey and show how this new version is built. We will offer detailed insights in our decision-making process, in new concepts and ideas and in the way we work. 

## What you'll get

You will get exclusive acccess to our process diary and wiki, as well as our code repositories. You will have a chance to play with the new UI Kit for Kirby's Panel first and you will be able to join our alpha and beta as the very first users. All supporters will also be invited to our private Slack channel and you'll receive weekly updates about our progress. In addition, Bastian is going to live-stream coding sessions on a regular basis, which you can join or rewatch later.
