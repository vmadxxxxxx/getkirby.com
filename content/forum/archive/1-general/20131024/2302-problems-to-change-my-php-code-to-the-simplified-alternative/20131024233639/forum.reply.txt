Text: There are two types of simplified if clauses. You're mixing the syntax for the other one with the ecco syntax. I think it's either:

    <? ecco($item->isOpen(), 'class="active"') ?>

or

    <?= ($item->isOpen()) ? 'class="active"': '') ?>
