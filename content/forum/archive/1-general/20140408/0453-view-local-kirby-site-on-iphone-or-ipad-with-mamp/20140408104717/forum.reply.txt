Text: If you set the url like this:

```php
c::set('url', false);
```

like in the default `config.php`, which will work in most situations, Kirby simply works and you don't have to think about it. :)

When using MAMP Pro 3, there is also a new `xip.io` option, which will use wildcard DNS to automatically route the site to your computer.
