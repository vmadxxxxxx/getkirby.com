Text: You can easily get the passed variables in the snippets by using `$varName`.

Example:

In `templates/default.php`:

```php
<header>
    <?php snippet('header', array('myVar' => 'myValue')); ?>
</header>
```

In `snippets/header.php`:

```php
<?php echo $myVar; // 'myValue' ?>
```
