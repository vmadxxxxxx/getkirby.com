Text: That's a limitation of Markdown. What you can do is to add image tags without a paragraph break: 

```
Sometext
(\image: myimage.jpg)

```

The image will be placed within the previous paragraph then. 

```
<p>
Sometext<br />
<img src="myimage.jpg" />
</p>
```
